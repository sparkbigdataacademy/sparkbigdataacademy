About Spark Big Data Academy

Our Vision, Your Success

Our Mission

At Spark Big Data Academy, our mission is to bridge the skill gap in the rapidly evolving Big Data industry. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, practical, and accessible training that empowers individuals to become proficient Big Data professionals and achieve their career aspirations.

Our Vision

To be the leading training academy for Big Data and Analytics in Nellore, India, recognized for our industry-relevant curriculum, expert instructors, and a strong track record of student success and placement.

Meet the Founder: M Madhuri Srilakshmi

M Madhuri Srilakshmi is the visionary Founder of Spark Big Data Academy. With 10 years of experience in the Big Data and Analytics domain, she has worked on complex data projects, led data teams, expertise in specific big data tools and technologies.

Madhuri's passion lies in empowering the next generation of data professionals. She recognized the growing demand for skilled Big Data talent and founded Spark Big Data Academy to provide hands-on, industry-aligned training that truly prepares students for real-world challenges.

Her commitment to practical learning and fostering a deep understanding of Big Data concepts is at the core of the academy's philosophy.
